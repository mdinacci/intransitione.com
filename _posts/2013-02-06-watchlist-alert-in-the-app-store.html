My first app on the sale in the Mac App Store is a simple utility app to monitor one's eBay watchlist...

---

I developed a simple utility application called Watchlist Alert which monitors a user's eBay watchlist and notify him when the items in his watchlist are due to expire.

It started as an app to "scratch an itch" as at one point I was using eBay a lot and I was constantly forgetting about the bids I was watching. With a little bit more effort I polished and published it in the Mac App Store.

I'm planning to distribute it outside the Apple store too, mostly to see how complicated the whole process it.

If you happen to be using it and have any comments or suggestions, please leave them on the support website or just fire me an e-mail from the contact page.
